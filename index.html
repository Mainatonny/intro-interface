Emergency Assist App

Your quick help in emergencies, right at your fingertips

Service Description

This app is designed to help users quickly get help during emergency situations. Whether you experience an impact, press your power/volume buttons multiple times, or shake your phone quickly, the app detects the event and sends a push notification to nearby users. These users can then report the incident to hospitals or police, and once the reporter uploads evidence of the report, they are rewarded.

The app is available as both a free and paid subscription service. Free users have options to pay for premium services or extend their subscription by earning points through promotions. Additionally, users can participate in scratch lotteries to win free prizes, and promotional partners can earn revenue based on app referrals.

Key Functionalities

1. Function Definition

  • Shock Detection & Notification: Automatic push notifications are sent when the user shocks the phone (via impact, rapid button presses, or fast shakes). Notifications alert other users who can then report the incident.
  • Reporting & Rewards: After a report is submitted and evidence is uploaded, reporters receive rewards. The reward value is deducted from the amount pre-paid by the reporter, and these rewards can be used for paid services.
  • Subscription Service: A dual free/paid subscription model where free users can either pay for premium features or earn points via promotions to extend the service period.
  • Promotional Partner Features: Users can register as promotional partners and earn a percentage of sales generated from their promotional activities.
  • Scratch Lottery Feature: Offers free prizes by letting users scratch lottery tickets; prizes may include products or in-app benefits.
